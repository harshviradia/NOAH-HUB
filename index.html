House Price Prediction

Sroll To Bottom

ABSTARCT

House prices increase every year, so there is a need for a system to predict house prices in the future. House price prediction can help the developer determine the selling price of a house and can help the customer to arrange the right time to purchase a house. There are three factors that influence the price of a house which include physical conditions, concept and location. This research aims to predict house prices based on NJOP houses in Malang city with regression analysis and particle swarm optimization (PSO). PSO is used for selection of affect variables and regression analysis is used to determine the optimal coefficient in prediction.
